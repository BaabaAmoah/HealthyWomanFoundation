Does weightloss tea really work?


You’ve probably seen a lot of Instagram ads about weight loss pills, tea, etc. Well, you’re probably thinking do these things actually work? Let’s dive in, shall we?

  • Teas have a type of flavonoid called catechins that may boost metabolism
    and help your body break down fats more
    quickly. And the caffeine in many teas increases your energy use,
    causing your body to burn more calories. These two compounds probably aid weight loss.
    Most of these ‘detox’ teas often work as diuretics which in a way makes you lose a lot of water from your body.
    Loss of water weight often looks like significant weight loss in a short period.

  • However these products can cause dehydration, diarrhea,
    flushing out of good bacteria, changes in menstrual cycles
    and to some extent kidney and liver problems after long term use.
    This teas and pills may or May not lead to short-term weight loss
    Meaning you are going to GAIN the weight you lost initially

  • Instead of focusing on miraculous products, we should instead focus
    on our overall health and wellbeing which involves a balanced diet,
    positive lifestyle choices such as reducing alcohol intake,
    and exercise. For those who want to really lose weight you might want to get into a calorie deficit,
    which will be up our next article.
